3D Printing Textiles:

A Fun and Creative Event for Kids

Embark on a hands-on adventure where young minds explore the intersection of technology and textiles. Children engage with 3D printing technologies, bringing their imaginative designs to life as they witness the magic of creating wearable art. This interactive experience sparks curiosity and encourages the next generation of designers to envision the future of fashion.

Crafts and Creations

Activity station where families can make their own textile-based crafts, such as weaving or tie-dyeing.

Families unite at this vibrant station, weaving memories through textile-based crafts. From intricate weaving projects to vibrant tie-dye creations, participants unleash their artistic flair. This collaborative space fosters a sense of community and showcases the enduring joy of crafting, emphasizing the timeless allure of textiles as a medium for self-expression.

Fashion Show

Featuring historical and contemporary textile designs

Step into a runway that spans centuries, where the past and present converge in a spectacular display of textile artistry. Marvel at the evolution of fashion through a curated showcase of historical garments alongside contemporary designs. This fashion extravaganza narrates the rich tapestry of textile history, celebrating the enduring creativity that weaves together diverse eras and styles.
