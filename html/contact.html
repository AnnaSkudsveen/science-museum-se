Where to find us

Mølleparken 4, 0459 Oslo

Take the Line 11 tram from Jernbanetorget to Birkelunden. The tram ride takes approximately 14 minutes, walk 5 minutes to Mølleparken 4. Alternatively, you can take the Line 34 bus from Jernbanetorget to Alexander Kiellands plass. The bus ride takes around 15 minutes , from Alexander Kiellands plass, walk to Mølleparken 4. If you prefer walking, it will take approximately 28 minutes from Oslo Central Station to Mølleparken 41.
