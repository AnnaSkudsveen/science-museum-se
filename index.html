New exhibit

The Future of Textile Tech: 3D Printing, Wearable Tech, and Beyond

Explore the cutting edge of textile technology, where innovation knows no bounds. From the precision of 3D printing to the seamless integration of wearable tech, the future of textiles is a realm of endless possibilities. Fabrics are no longer just materials; they are dynamic interfaces merging style and functionality. Step into a world where design meets digital, and the boundaries between fashion and technology dissolve, heralding a new era in the evolution of textile tech.

25. November - 31. December

3D Printing Christmas ornaments: A Fun Event for Kids

Calling all young inventors and textile enthusiasts! Get ready for an exciting journey into the world of 3D printing textiles. Our 3D Printing Textiles Event at the Oslo Science Museum is not just a showcase—it’s an interactive experience that lets kids become designers and creators.

Event Details: Dates: November 25 to December 31, 2023 Age Group: Suitable for kids aged 7 and above Admission: Free for museum members, 50 NOK for non-members

Where to find us

Take the Line 11 tram from Jernbanetorget to Birkelunden. The tram ride takes approximately 14 minutes, walk 5 minutes to Mølleparken 4.
